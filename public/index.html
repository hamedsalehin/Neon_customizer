Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about designing, shipping, and mounting your Nano Sign.

Q: How much does a custom sign cost? +
Custom sign pricing depends entirely on the size of the board and total length of the neon tubes. You can use our interactive Neon Customizer tool to view immediate pricing for your specific text, fonts, and sizes.
Q: What certifications do your neon signs have? +
All our signs and power adapters are CE, UL, and RoHS certified. We use low-voltage 12V outputs, ensuring the signs produce no noise, do not heat up, and are completely safe to handle and install.
Q: How can I install or mount my sign? +
Every sign ships with pre-drilled holes in the acrylic backing and a full mounting kit. You can hang it from the ceiling with chains, mount it flush against walls like a picture frame, or display it on a stand.
Q: Are your neon lights waterproof? +
By default, our customizer signs are designed for indoor use. However, we offer waterproof options (rated IP67) for outdoor commercial usage upon request.
Q: What is included in my shipping box? +
Your package will include the custom LED sign, a power supply adapter matching your regional wall socket, a wireless dimmer remote controller to adjust brightness levels, and wall-mounting/hanging hardware.
