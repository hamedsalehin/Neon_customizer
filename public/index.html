Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about designing, customizing, and mounting your custom LED neon sign.

Q: How much does a custom LED neon sign cost and what factors affect pricing? +
The cost of a custom LED neon sign or personalized neon logo is calculated dynamically based on your unique design specifications. When you design your own neon sign online using our interactive custom neon sign creator, pricing is computed in real-time based on several key factors: (1) The total linear length of the LED neon flex tubing needed to form your custom text, script font, quotes, or corporate branding logo—meaning character count, font style, and overall size directly influence the final cost; (2) The surface area and complexity of the acrylic backing board, which we offer in transparent, solid black, mirror silver, or custom color-printed styles; and (3) Advanced design upgrades, including IP67 waterproof sealant treatment for outdoor business signs, multi-color RGB color-changing controller kits, or direct UV printing integration for intricate, multi-colored business logo details. Unlike traditional glass neon signs that require high-voltage transformers and specialized glassblowers, our modern, low-voltage 12V LED custom sign fabrication is highly cost-effective. This allows us to manufacture luxury wedding neon signs, customized room lights, business lobby signage, and commercial storefront LED signs that fit a wide range of budgets. Start designing with our live visual customizer tool to preview sizes, fonts, colors, and get instant pricing.
Q: What certifications and safety ratings do your modern LED neon signs possess? +
Yes, safety is the primary priority in our sign design and manufacturing process. All of our custom LED neon signs, silicone LED flex modules, and low-voltage power supply adapters are fully certified to meet UL-listed safety standards, CE compliance certifications, and RoHS environmental guidelines. Traditional glass neon lights operate on heavy, hot, high-voltage transformers (often ranging from 3,000 to 15,000 volts) that generate considerable heat, emit an audible buzzing noise, consume excessive electricity, and present a risk of fire or dangerous mercury vapor leaks if the glass breaks. In contrast, our modern silicone-based LED neon flex alternative operates on a safe, low-voltage 12V DC power input. This means our signs remain completely cool to the touch even after weeks of continuous 24/7 commercial operation, run silently without any hum, contain no toxic gases, and are housed in high-durability, shatterproof silicone casings. This makes our LED light signs ideal and perfectly safe for low-clearance residential wall decor, kids' bedrooms, home bars, high-traffic retail storefronts, tradeshow displays, and corporate offices.
Q: How do I install, mount, or hang my custom neon sign? +
Installing and mounting your custom LED neon sign is a quick, DIY-friendly process that takes less than 15 minutes. Every custom sign is pre-drilled with multiple structural mounting holes in the premium 5mm cast acrylic backing plate and ships with a complete, professional installation kit. Depending on your storefront window, corporate lobby, office wall, or home decor layout, you can choose from three versatile installation methods:

1. Stainless Steel Wall Standoffs: Perfect for wall mounting, this hardware mounts the acrylic sign securely flush or slightly offset from the wall, creating a luxurious "floating" 3D shadow effect that is highly popular for corporate office branding, reception desk signs, and bedroom wall decor.
2. Ceiling Hanging Chains: Designed for hanging installations, this setup uses high-strength metal suspension chains through the top pre-drilled holes to hang the sign in windows, commercial storefront doors, or event backdrops to capture foot traffic.
3. Countertop Acrylic Stand: We can fabricate a flat acrylic pedestal stand base, allowing you to display your sign directly on tables, bar tops, checkout counters, reception desks, or exhibition booths without drilling any holes.

To activate the sign, simply mount it in place, plug it into the low-voltage 12V power supply adapter, and plug the adapter into any standard wall outlet for an instant glowing display.
Q: What backing options do you offer for custom logos and quote signs? +
We provide a variety of high-grade backing materials, cuts, and finishes to ensure your custom sign matches your interior design or brand logo guidelines. Our backing plates are crafted from heavy-duty 5mm thick cast acrylic sheets and are available in three distinct cuts:

1. Cut-to-Shape (Contoured): The acrylic backing is precisely routed to follow the exact contours of the glowing neon letters or logo outline. This is our most popular option because it provides a clean, minimal look where the backing remains virtually invisible once illuminated.
2. Whole Board (Solid Panel): The backing is cut as a solid rectangle, square, or circle. This is highly recommended for commercial signs, large business logos, storefront window signs, and signs that need to be mounted on event grid walls or staged at weddings.
3. Stand-Mounted Base: The backing plate is anchored to a flat acrylic stand, making it portable and easy to place on flat surfaces like desks or bars.

We also offer several backing color finishes, including crystal-clear transparent acrylic (for a seamless floating look), solid glossy black (which enhances neon color contrast), mirror silver (for a high-end luxury reflective look), and custom color-printed backdrops for direct UV printed graphics.
Q: What is included in the package when I order a Nano Neon sign? +
Every custom LED sign shipment from Nano Sign is a complete, pre-configured plug-and-play package with no assembly or extra purchases required. Inside your custom-engineered, foam-lined shipping carton, you will find: (1) Your Handcrafted LED Neon Sign custom-built using high-intensity SMD LEDs encased in UV-resistant silicone; (2) Low-Voltage Power Supply (12V) matching your regional outlet standards (UL-certified power adapter with a total cable reach of over 10 feet); (3) Wireless Remote Control & Dimmer that lets you adjust the sign's brightness from a soft 10% glow to a vibrant 100% brightness, as well as switch between dynamic flashing, pulsing, fading, and speed modes; and (4) Heavy-Duty Installation Kit including solid stainless steel wall standoff cylinders, wall plugs, screws, and a set of ceiling hanging chains.
Q: Can I turn my business logo or custom graphic design into a neon sign? +
Yes, translating complex brand logos, corporate graphics, custom artwork, and specific brand typography into custom neon signs is our specialty. To achieve absolute visual accuracy and match your brand guidelines, we combine two advanced technologies: (1) Flexible LED Neon Tubing for the main glowing outline contours and bold text paths; and (2) Direct UV Printing (Ultra-Violet Cured Ink) on the acrylic backing plate for fine details, complex shading, gradients, and small text that cannot be formed out of physical neon tubing. This hybrid manufacturing method allows us to duplicate any corporate logo design exactly, complete with true-to-brand color matching. To get a design mock-up and a detailed price estimate, visit our Upload Logo / Get Quote page and upload your file (SVG, AI, PDF, EPS, or high-res PNG). Our designers will generate a free digital proof and price quote for you within 24 hours.
Q: Are your LED neon signs suitable for outdoor use? Can they handle rain? +
While our standard custom neons are designed for indoor environments, we offer professional IP67-rated waterproof upgrades specifically engineered for outdoor commercial storefronts, building facades, open-air events, and restaurant signage. Outdoor signs undergo a specialized sealing process where all electrical connections, end-caps, and wiring joints are treated with marine-grade waterproof sealant. The LED flex tubing is housed in thick, IP67-rated weather-resistant silicone sleeves, and the sign ships with a fully sealed, waterproof outdoor power adapter. This ensures your sign can safely withstand heavy rain, snow, high humidity, and prolonged exposure to direct sunlight without losing brightness or short-circuiting. If you plan to install your sign outdoors, please check the outdoor box when requesting a custom quote on our Get Quote page.
